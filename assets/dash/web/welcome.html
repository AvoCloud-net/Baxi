WHAT BAXI CAN DO

Features

Everything you need to run a great Discord server - configured in minutes from this dashboard.

Moderation

Ban, kick, mute, warn - with temp durations, auto-escalation and DM notifications.

Chat Filter

Three-layer filtering: keyword/regex rules, AI-powered content moderation and real-time phishing URL detection.

Anti-Spam

Real-time detection of message floods, mention spam and repeated content.

Welcomer

Custom join/leave messages with images, colors and member variables.

Live Stats Channels

Voice channels that display member count, bots, roles and more - updated automatically.

Livestream Tracker

Twitch, YouTube and TikTok go-live notifications with live viewer counts, embed previews and optional role pings.

Global Chat

Cross-server chat rooms with 24/7 moderation and a shared flagged users database.

Temp Voice Channels

Auto-create and delete voice channels when members join a trigger channel.

Support Tickets

Member support system with staff roles, transcripts and a full dashboard log.

Prism - Trust Scoring

Automated behavior analysis. Every user gets a trust score based on violations across all Baxi servers - bad actors get flagged automatically.

Minigames

Counting Game and Flag Quiz - keep your community engaged with built-in leaderboards and configurable hints.

Suggestions

Members post ideas in a dedicated channel - Baxi turns them into embeds with Accept / Decline buttons for staff.

Reaction & Button Roles

Let members assign themselves roles by clicking a button - fully configured from the dashboard.

Auto-Roles

Automatically assign one or more roles to every new member when they join the server.

Custom Commands

Create your own slash commands with custom text or embed responses - no coding required.

Warnings & Auto-Slowmode

Issue warnings with DM notifications and let Baxi automatically throttle channels when activity spikes.

Verification

Button-based member verification with a customisable panel - keep bots and raiders out automatically.

Sticky Messages

Pin a message to the bottom of any channel - Baxi re-posts it automatically whenever other messages push it up.

YouTube Video Alerts

Get notified the moment a tracked YouTube channel uploads - Baxi posts an embed with title, thumbnail and an optional role ping.

Leveling System

Members earn XP for every message and level up automatically - configure role rewards for milestone levels directly from the dashboard.

Giveaway

Start timed giveaways with a button - Baxi picks random winners, announces them in channel and notifies them by DM.

Polls

Create polls with up to 10 answers, an optional duration and an image - members vote with buttons and results update live.

Auto-Release

Automatically publish messages in announcement channels - every news post is pushed to all following servers without manual action.

Music Player

Play YouTube, SoundCloud and live radio in voice channels - queue, skip, volume and per-server source whitelisting.

DiscordGate

Link Minecraft accounts to Discord - verified members get auto-roles, in-game perks and join announcements in your server.
